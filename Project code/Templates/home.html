About This Tool

What is Fetal Health Classification?

Fetal health is a critical concern during pregnancy. Our AI-powered application helps healthcare providers and expectant parents understand fetal well-being by analyzing cardiotocogram (CTG) data.

Using advanced machine learning techniques, specifically a Random Forest algorithm trained on thousands of CTG records, we can predict fetal health status with high accuracy.

Why It Matters?

Early detection of fetal health issues can:

  • ✅ Enable timely medical intervention
  • ✅ Reduce maternal and fetal complications
  • ✅ Improve pregnancy outcomes
  • ✅ Provide peace of mind to parents

Prediction Classes

✅

Normal (Class 1)

Fetal health is normal with healthy cardiac condition, normal variability, and no concerning decelerations detected.

⚠️

Suspect (Class 2)

Some concerning indicators detected. Further clinical evaluation and monitoring are recommended by healthcare providers.

❌

Pathological (Class 3)

Serious abnormalities detected in cardiotocogram readings. Immediate clinical attention and medical intervention may be required.
